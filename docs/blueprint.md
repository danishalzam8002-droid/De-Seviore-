# **App Name**: De'Seviore

## Core Features:

- Batch Overview Page: A dynamic main page showcasing the batch's history, philosophy, official logo, and curated galleries of images and videos from class events and activities.
- Member Profile Listing: A browsable page displaying all class members, featuring their name and photo, with links to individual, detailed profiles.
- Individual Member Profiles: Dedicated pages for each class member, showing their photo, full name, place and date of birth, contact information (phone, Instagram), and a personal 'kata-kata' or quote.
- Member Data Administration: A secure, authenticated section for administrators to create, update, or delete member profiles, including uploading their photos and editing all associated personal details.
- Main Content Administration: An authenticated administrative interface for updating the batch's history and philosophy text, and for uploading/managing the image and video galleries displayed on the Batch Overview Page.
- Admin Authentication: A robust login system to control and restrict access to the content and member data management features to authorized administrators only.
- Quote Suggestion Tool: An AI-powered tool available during member profile editing to suggest inspirational or reflective quotes ('kata-kata') based on administrator-provided keywords or themes, enhancing member biographies.

## Style Guidelines:

- A dark color scheme to emphasize media content and convey a sense of legacy and sophistication. The primary color is a rich, deep blue (#1F4799), symbolizing trust, depth, and stability, reminiscent of a strong community and lasting legacy.
- The background color is a highly desaturated variant of the primary hue (#1F232E), providing a subtle depth that allows other UI elements and content to stand out.
- An accent color of vibrant aqua (#1ACCE6) is used for calls to action, highlights, and interactive elements, providing a dynamic contrast and representing the joy and energy of shared experiences within the community.
- Headline font: 'Playfair' (serif), chosen for its elegant, high-contrast lines that evoke a sense of importance and refined heritage, suitable for conveying the batch's legacy and philosophy.
- Body text font: 'PT Sans' (humanist sans-serif), selected for its excellent readability and modern warmth, making member biographies and detailed historical texts engaging and comfortable to read.
- Utilize modern, clean, and easily recognizable icons that support content navigation and functional interactions, aligning with themes of community, media, and personal information.
- Employ a responsive and intuitive layout with distinct sections for history, galleries, and member profiles. Gallery layouts should be visually appealing (e.g., masonry grids for images, clear video showcases).
- For the member biography menu, display profiles in a paginated layout showing 3 members per page, with swipeable or navigable pages for browsing.
